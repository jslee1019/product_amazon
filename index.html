Why guarana caffeine beats coffee caffeine

Coffee delivers caffeine fast — a sharp peak in the bloodstream within 30–45 minutes, followed by an equally sharp drop. That drop is the crash. Guarana caffeine binds to natural tannins in the seed, which slow its release in the digestive tract. The result is a flatter, longer curve: less spike, no cliff.

What L-Theanine at 200mg actually does

Smooths the peak. L-Theanine crosses the blood-brain barrier and increases alpha-wave activity — the brain state associated with calm focus. When paired with caffeine at 2:1, it flattens the stimulant peak while preserving the alertness.

Extends the curve. Multiple clinical studies have shown that L-Theanine + caffeine at this ratio improves sustained attention, reaction time, and task-switching accuracy for up to 3–5 hours longer than caffeine alone.

For your team's briefing notes: The 2:1 formula isn't a marketing number. It's the exact ratio used in the most cited caffeine-theanine cognitive performance research (Owen et al., 2008; Giesbrecht et al., 2010). Creators and their audiences who've researched nootropics will recognize this instantly — it adds significant credibility versus generic "clean energy" claims.

10 / Messaging Guidelines

Do's & don'ts.

Rules that should be reflected in every creator brief the agency sends out. The FDA disclaimer in particular is non-negotiable for any post discussing health benefits.

+ Please Do
  • +Describe SANS as "beanless coffee" or "a new category of coffee."
  • +Emphasize the clean caffeine source (guarana, not coffee beans) for Focus & Energy.
  • +Use the 2:1 L-Theanine to caffeine ratio as the hero formula claim.
  • +Show the brewing process — the clean dissolve is a visual differentiator.
  • +Use natural lighting. Ceramic, glass, and minimal styling for the premium positioning.
  • +Lead with how the product feels — calm focus, smooth energy, no crash.
× Please Don't
  • ×Call SANS a "coffee alternative" or "instant coffee substitute" — it's a new category.
  • ×Position SANS as "caffeine-free" in Focus & Energy posts — it has 100mg.
  • ×Lump SANS in with mushroom coffee brands (Four Sigmatic, RYZE, MUD\WTR) — those all contain real coffee; SANS does not.
  • ×Claim SANS diagnoses, treats, cures, or prevents any disease.
  • ×Reference competitor brands by name or make direct comparisons in captions.
  • ×Make specific health claims not supported by on-pack labeling.
